How TrustScore works

Six weighted signals scored by algorithm, explained by your AI of choice.

01 / FEEDBACK
Volume & Rate
Scores both count and positivity rate. Below 98% positive triggers weighted penalties matching eBay dispute research.
02 / ACCOUNT
Account Age
New accounts under 90 days carry the highest fraud correlation. Graduated scoring from 0–15 pts.
03 / PRICING
Price Anomaly
Bidirectional — overpriced flags exploitation, underpriced flags counterfeits. Compared against sold history.
04 / LISTING
Listing Quality
Returns policy, photo authenticity, item location, description depth — composite of 5 sub-signals.
05 / DELIVERY
Delivery Record
eBay detailed seller ratings on delivery — lagging but reliable post-purchase dispute predictor.
06 / AI
AI Risk Summary
Plug in Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, or any local model. Structured signal data sent as context; natural language explanation returned.
